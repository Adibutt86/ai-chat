                      CHATBOX AI USER & SERVICE GUIDE
========================================================================

------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. PLATFORM OVERVIEW
------------------------------------------------------------------------
AICHAT is an all-in-one conversational AI platform designed to automate 
customer engagement and booking scheduling. The system embeds a beautiful, 
interactive chat bubble on your website, allowing visitors to get answers 
to their questions and schedule appointments directly within the chat window.

------------------------------------------------------------------------
2. BUSINESS CHANNELS & SERVICE FEATURES
------------------------------------------------------------------------
- Core Capabilities: The AI assistant is active 24/7. It provides visitors 
  with direct, friendly answers using knowledge crawled from your website, 
  uploaded documents, or custom FAQ pairs.
- Lead Capture System: When a visitor asks about pricing, quotes, or demo 
  services, the chatbot automatically prompts them to capture their email, 
  name, and contact information. Leads are saved instantly to the dashboard.
- Interactive Scheduling: Visitors can view list pricing, select standard 
  company services, and book open slots directly through the chat bubble.

------------------------------------------------------------------------
3. CALENDAR SYNCHRONIZATION & BOOKINGS
------------------------------------------------------------------------
- Real-Time Scheduling: The booking calendar is fully automated. When a 
  customer selects a date and slot, it registers in the backend console.
- Google Calendar Sync: If you connect your Google Calendar in your Settings, 
  scheduled slots are pushed automatically to your personal Google Calendar.
- Double-Booking Protection: The scheduling system automatically blocks out 
  times when you are busy on your Google Calendar, preventing overlapping slots.
- Status Controls: You can manage, confirm, complete, or cancel appointments 
  directly from your dashboard. Cancelled bookings are deleted automatically 
  from your connected Google Calendar.

------------------------------------------------------------------------
4. HOW TO CONNECT GOOGLE CALENDAR
------------------------------------------------------------------------
To connect your Google Calendar to the AICHAT system:
1. Log in to your AICHAT dashboard console.
2. Go to the Settings tab in the sidebar.
3. Click the "Connect Google Calendar" button.
4. Grant the requested permissions in the Google authorization window.
5. Choose your preferred calendar from the dropdown selection list.
6. Once linked, the system will sync your appointments and busy times.

------------------------------------------------------------------------
5. FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS (FAQs)
------------------------------------------------------------------------
- Question: How do I book an appointment with you?
  Answer: You can book an appointment directly here in the chat! Just click the "Book an Appointment" button or type "I want to book an appointment" to choose a service, date, and time slot.

- Question: What services do you offer?
  Answer: We offer professional consultation services, custom AI agent setups, and website integrations. You can view all our current services and prices directly in the booking menu.

- Question: How do I connect my Google Calendar?
  Answer: Log in to your dashboard, go to the Settings tab, click "Connect Google Calendar", and grant the necessary permissions. Once connected, select your calendar from the dropdown list.

- Question: Do you have a free plan?
  Answer: Yes! We offer a free plan for developers and small websites that includes up to 15 chat requests per minute using the free tier.

- Question: When are you open?
  Answer: Our official working hours are Monday to Friday from 09:00 to 17:00 (UTC). We are closed on weekends.
